Home Axes

A home position is a known, absolute physical location on the cutting table. From this screen, you can move each axis to a home position. You can also send the transverse and rail axes to one of 12 pre-programmed alternate home positions from this screen.

Navigation: Main screen > Manual > Home Axes

On a cutting machine that has tilt and rotate axes, when you choose the Home Axes soft key, Phoenix displays a message that asks if you want to home the tilt and rotate axes.

  • Choose Yes to continue to the Home Axes screen. Phoenix displays the Tilt and Rotate soft keys at the bottom of the screen so that you can home those axes.
  • Choose No to continue to the Home Axes screen. Phoenix displays the Transverse, Rail, and THC soft keys at the bottom of the screen so that you can home those axes.

Field Definitions

Home Position

Choose the X/Y Home Position that you want to define. You can define up to 12 X/Y home positions. Enter the X and Y positions for each home position.

Following Error

Following error is the difference between the commanded motor position and the actual motor position as calculated by the drive. A large following error may indicate that the cut speed may be higher than the cutting system is capable of moving.

Soft Keys

Go to Home Position 1 – 12

This soft key moves the axis/axes to the home position that you have defined for that soft key.

Transverse, Rail, THC

This soft key homes the individual axis.

Rotate, Tilt

This soft key homes the individual axis.

All

This soft key moves either the Transverse, Rail, and THC axes or the Rotate and Tilt axes to their home positions.

Watch Window Selections

These soft keys allow you to select the Watch Window that you want to see. You can view up to 10 different Watch screens.

Choose the center soft key to select and see a specific Watch Window number.

Choose the left (arrow) or right (arrow) to select and see the previous Watch Window or the next one.
