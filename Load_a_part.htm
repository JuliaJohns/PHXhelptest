Load a part

Load a Simple Shape

  1. From the Main screen, choose Shape Manager.

  2. Choose one of the green, blue, or black shapes, then choose OK.

  3. In the next screen, you can make the following adjustments:

    • Adjust the part dimensions.

    • Select whether to position the lead-in and lead-out on the inside (hole) or the outside (piece) of the part.

    • Select the type of leads.

    • Choose additional options depending on the shape.

  4. Choose OK to proceed to ProNest CNC to select the quantity of parts and the process used to cut the simple shape.

  5. Complete making your selections in ProNest CNC.

  6. Choose OK to load the part and return to the Main screen, or choose Cancel to cancel ProNest CNC and to proceed loading a part without process information.

Load a Part From the Hard Drive

  1. From the Main screen, choose Files.
  2. In Load From, choose the folder where the part program resides.
  3. Choose the part program from the Files list. If the Preview box is checked, the part shows in the Preview window.
  4. Choose OK to load the part and return to the Main screen.

Notes:

  • Phoenix shows the parts with extension that you have specified in the Special Setups screen.
  • DXF and DWG files cannot be loaded from the Files screen in Phoenix. Use ProNest CNC to load DXF and DWG files.
