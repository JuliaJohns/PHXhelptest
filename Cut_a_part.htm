CutPro Wizard

CutPro Wizard simplifies the part and process selection sequence for cutting a part. You can launch the CutPro Wizard by choosing the CutPro Wizard soft key on the Main screen. You can also set the CutPro Wizard to open automatically by enabling Automatic CutPro Wizard in the Status/Message or Wizard list in the Special Setups screen. CutPro Wizard guides you through these steps:

  • Load a part.
  • Select a cutting process.
  • Align the part to the workpiece.
  • Verify the consumable set.
  • Start cutting the part.

Trial or Cut a Part

You can trial a part to verify machine motion without cutting.

  1. From the Main screen, choose Change Cut Mode to toggle between trial mode and each of the available cut modes. Stop when the cut mode you want is shown on the Main screen.
  2. Make sure that the Kerf and Cut Speed are set appropriately for the part.
  3. Press Start on the screen or the EDGE Connect operator console, or F9 on the keyboard. Phoenix may display some messages before cutting begins. For example, you may see a prompt to home the cutting machine, the torch height control, or to verify that you are ready for the cutting machine to begin motion.
  4. To pause the part program, press Stop on the screen or the operator console, or press F10 on the keyboard.

 

You can restart the program by pressing Start or F9 again.
